So, Why Should We Care?

Do Yourself A Favor

Pollution in our city has many negative side effects on our health and the environment, and it impacts our quality of life. There are ongoing efforts aiming to create a cleaner and safer environment for all New Yorkers.

How Pollution Impacts Us

The Hudson River is contaminated with chemicals and waste products such as polychlorinated biphenyls (PCBs), heavy metals, sewage, and many kinds of bacteria. This affects local marine life and, in turn, creates many problems for people and industries.

Air quality can also vary throughout the year. Air pollution contributes to many respiratory illnesses and hospitalization in our metropolitan area.
