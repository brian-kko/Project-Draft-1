Cleaning Up NYC

Being the most populated city in the country, New York is also regarded as one of the dirtiest.
Areas within our city can vary in levels of sanitation.
Trash and litter on the streets are a common occurrence.
We, as New Yorkers, should strive to keep our city, air, and waterways clean.
